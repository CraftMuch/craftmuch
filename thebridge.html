The Bridge

The goal in Bedwars is to destroy the opposing teams beds while protecting your own. While your teams bed is intact, you get infinite respawns whenever you die. However, if your bed is destroyed, you will get eliminated instantly when you die, so protect your bed! Collect iron and gold and buy items to prepare yourself for battle, while you can use diamonds to give yourself enchantments like protection, or buy traps that will be activated when someone tries to destroy your bed. You can get better items than gold or iron using emeralds. You can also buy obsidian to make breaking your bed much harder.

Last team standing wins!
