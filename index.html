About us

Minigames for all kinds of appetites, CraftMuch is a Minecraft Bedrock Edition server, whose main goal is to entertain its players and prove that expensive plugins are not needed to make a fantastic server. Being family-friendly, CraftMuch is suited to all age types. Playing on CraftMuch is completely free - all upgrades inside the server can be bought using an in-game currency (called CraftCoins) which means that no real life money would be spent. Having a friendly staff team, we provide the support you need playing on the server. With constant updates, we fix issues as soon as they are found, and we make sure our players never get bored by releasing new minigames.
